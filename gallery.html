Our Community in Action

Moments of connection, growth, and support from our workshops, peer counseling sessions, and community events.

These photos capture the heart of Youth Arise: young people supporting each other, learning new skills, and building a community where mental health conversations are normal and welcomed. Every workshop, every counseling session, and every event brings us closer to a generation that prioritizes mental wellness.

Art Therapy Session

Using creative expression as a tool for processing emotions and building self-awareness.
